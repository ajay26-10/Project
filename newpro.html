STORY

Take your mobile photography experience to the next level with SwitchLens - instantly transform any smartphone into a professional camera via Wi-Fi with interchangeable lenses. With its user-friendly design, all you need to do is snap it on to your smartphone, and you're ready to start capturing the most remarkable, visually stunning photographs and videos.

Interchangeable Lens

SwitchLens is designed around the versatile micro four thirds (M43, M4/3, MFT) system, providing you with an impressive arsenal of lenses to select from. Whether you're shooting landscape, portrait, or any scene that requires a specific visual style, you can easily choose a lens that perfectly aligns with your needs and the desired effect. The possibilities are limitless. The best part is you can use all your existing M43 compatible lenses (eg. Panasonic / Olympus) with SwitchLens. Another advantage of this system is the lens sizes are smaller and lighter in weight, making it easier to carry on your travels.

Wireless Preview and Control

SwitchLens connects to your smartphone through Wi-Fi, allowing it to operate without physically attaching to your phone. This feature gives you the flexibility to position it at any angle, enabling you to capture challenging shots. Its design encourages creative photography and is perfect for taking selfies.

Detachable Screen

Experience the highest quality selfies you’ve ever taken. SwitchLens allows you to capture selfies with the best camera available. And you’ll be able to easily check all the details before every snap.

3X Larger Screen

Take full advantage of your smartphone’s full screen to preview every shot before you capture. This will eliminate any unnecessary retakes and save you time, so you will never miss the moment.

Lightweight and Pocket Sized

Weighing only 180g/6.3oz (200g/7oz with battery), SwitchLens is super lightweight and extremely portable. You can take it everywhere you go so you will always be ready to capture life’s funniest and most important experiences. The 14500 rechargeable battery is AA sized and can conveniently be found online or electronic stores and camera outlets everywhere.

Research and Development

About

Sneaki Design was formed by a group of friends who shared a vision to create gadgets for taking with you everywhere. Their first attempt, the Halohub, did not go well, but learned some valuable lessons. After a two year hiatus they are back with a new project. With Ivan, electronic engineer and amateur photographer, leading the charge, the team has created SwitchLens to disrupt and blur the line between mobile and professional photography. Of course, doing such a project is not easy. Learning from their previous mistakes, the team understood they needed more initial feedback and also strong partners in the industry. For a wider range of feedback, they were at exhibitions in April gathering criticism. Then, the team also found reliable and experienced partners on the manufacturing side. With these improvements, they have set up SwitchLens to be a success.
